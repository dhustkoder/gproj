Documentation on development on gproj.


Note: 
This project aims for a very simple game with a very simple angine that targets for good performance even on old systems;
Platform specific code goes only on src/$(platform), The rest must be portable.


1 Map:

Mapping is done with a limited subset of TMX format.
The first 2 layers are known as BG and BG2 layers, used for sprites that appears behind Actors (animated character sprites).
The other 2 layers are known as FG and FG2 layers, used for sprites that appears on top of Actors.
